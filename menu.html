Pork Menu

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Pork
  • 2 tablespoon garlic
  • 5 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 4 tablespoon vinegar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • oil
  • salt to taste

Procedure

  1. Put oil in frying pan and heat.
  2. Combined porl, soy sauce and garlic, marinate for at least one hour.
  3. Heat the pot and put-in marinated pork. Pan fry until brown.
  4. Pour remaining marinade including garlic.
  5. Add water, whole peppercorn, dried bay leaves, sugar and other seasoning.
  6. Put-in vinegar and simmer for 12-15 minutes.
  7. Serve hot ang Enjoy!
